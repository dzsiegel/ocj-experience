Sources

Listed below are all oral histories I used from Cal State Fullerton’s Lawrence de Graaf Center for Oral and Public History. Many are available online. I requested a handful of digitized recordings and transcriptions of interviews from the COPH directly, which are referenced without a URL since they are unavailable online. Also included are a couple outside sources that have helped in my research process.
